Please tell us in details about what you've been working on this summer. What kind of work did you do? What did you accomplish?


I interned at Google and worked for Feedback team. My project involves setting up backend infrastructure that allows real time status updates for Google Feedback team. My project helped with decreasing freshness delays of feedback updates and consolidating different bug storage schemas.





Please tell us several things you found most interesting/fun/rewarding and most challenging about what you did.


Learn about Google culture, especially get to know how a big tech company is organized and how do each team work with each other.

Be able to meet many women in tech and listen to their stories.




What are your biggest takeaways from what you did during the summer? It could be technical/social skills you learnt; could be that you finally found your passion; could be some thoughts about your life, etc.


Through the summer, I met a lot of talented engineers who are not only good at coding, but also have passion for things outside work. We discuss technical issues together, but we also go hiking, go to concerts and do all the fun stuff together. I think the people I met are the biggest takeaways from the summer and I'm looking forward to work with them again after graduation.
